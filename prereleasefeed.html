Introducing Wavebox - the next generation of WMail

5th April 2017

Available to download now from Wavebox.io

Read the full article on our new Blog

I'm super excited to finally share this with all the pre-release users! We've been hard at work over the past few weeks getting something really awesome ready and released. That something is the next generation of WMail, we've called it Wavebox.

WMail has grown from a 3-day vacation project into something too big for me to maintain in my free time. That said, I'm still focused on evolving it into something that works even better for myself and everyone who uses it. To move WMail forwards, I'm now very proud and excited to announce that it is now part of Bookry, an amazing company that I've been part of for the last 5 years.

Myself and the team at Bookry have been busy adding more features and improving Wavebox which builds on all the great features of WMail and adds so many more! The big one right out of the box is support for more than just Gmail & Inbox...

Thats right, as well as Google Inbox & Gmail, Wavebox brings Outlook, Office 365, Slack, Trello & Any Web link to the family. Meaning you have more online tools right at your fingertips.

That's not all though. Wavebox is jam-packed with more features! Here's a brief overview of some of the other new things that that come in Wavebox...

  • Better Google Services integration
  • Integrations with Microsoft Services
  • Google Docs & Office editing
  • Services toolbar for those of you with lots of accounts
  • Sleeping services & accounts to save you oodles of memory. Find out more.
  • Supercharged Google Notifications
  • Automatic updates on macOS & Windows (linux coming soon...)
  • Signed Binaries for easier install & added security
  • Still open-source on Github.

If you want to find out more, we have an excellent Blog post introducing all the new features in Wavebox. Once you're ready to get started and hit the road you'll need to head over to our new Website...

Afterwards you can continue using Wavebox basic for free or you can upgrade to Pro at any time. Wavebox also comes with a generous affiliate scheme so you can get Wavebox Pro for free & much more!
